BAHAY ARUGA

(A FREE HALFWAY HOUSE FOR PEDIATRIC CANCER PATIENT)

SERVICES

Temporary Shelter

A temporary shelter offers shortterm accommodation and basic services for cancer patients, providing a safe and supportive environment.

Day Care

A day care that provides services to children afflicted with different kinds of cancer and provide them with necessary support.

Activities

An activities for cancer patients can include gentle exercises, art and music therapy, meditation and mindfulness sessions, support groups, educational workshops, and recreational outings.

About Us

Bahay Aruga is a dedicated sanctuary offering free shelter to pediatric cancer patients and one parent or guardian. Founded in January 2014 by Ms. Mayette Bonilla, our mission is rooted in compassion and fairness, ensuring every child and their family receive the utmost care and support during their treatment journey. At Bahay Aruga, we believe in the power of a nurturing environment to aid in the healing process.
